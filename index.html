ABOUT ME

HI. As you probably already know, my name is Allan Kwan, and I am an interaction + user experience designer. Hopefully, you've already had a chance to look at the projects I've either worked on personally or greatly admired. But let's get back to the "About Me" part of this section. I am currently studying as an undergraduate at UC San Diego, working towards a B.S. in Cognitive Science, specializing in HCI. Initially, I went in as an Economics major, hoping to pursue business in the future. However, as a thriving photographer, I began to see that beauty can be reflected through multiple forms of media. And that's when I found Cognitive Science HCI, a field that reflected the combined beauty of the mind and design. So have fun, take a look around, and enjoy design for all it's worth.
